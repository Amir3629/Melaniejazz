About Me

As a professional jazz vocalist and experienced vocal coach in Berlin, I bring both performance expertise and teaching passion to every lesson. My approach combines technical excellence with creative expression, helping singers find their unique voice in jazz.

With years of stage experience and a deep understanding of vocal technique, I guide students through their musical journey - from basic foundations to advanced jazz improvisation.
